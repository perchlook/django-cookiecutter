Services We Offer

Tell us about your vision, and let us build it for you.

Strategic Planning and Operating Cadence

From Strategy Planning to Business Reviews, Perch works with you to design your ideal operating cadence. We'll work with your leaders and team members to make sure they are included in the process.

Don't worry, you and your teams can continue using the tools that you already love - let us orchestrate them to work for you.

End-to-End People and Talent Strategy

Deepen the capabilities that matter most - your people. We work with experienced HR leaders to develop a talent strategy that is aligned with your goals. Goal Tracking and Performance Management are critical feedback loops for all organizations, but they are often out of sync with each other. Perch can help bridge the gap and keep them in sync with your operating cadence.

Design Thinking and Technology Transformation

Teams overwhelmed by manual administrative work? Perch can embed design thinking and automation capabilities into your organization so that they proactively solve business problems, instead of delivering intiatives that the business needed a year ago.

Team Collaboration and Interface Design

Do you have a culture of accountability and collaboration to enable you to execute effectively? Teams often need to collaborate with other teams to deliver critical business results. We use Design and Systems Thinking to craft team interfaces that allow your teams to work cohesively even as your organization grows.

HR Transformation

Many HR teams are stuck with administrative work and are unable to meet the ever changing needs of an organisation. Perch helps HR teams move away from transactional administrative work by equipping the team with Technology, Design Thinking, and Systems Thinking.

Our HR maturity model allows us to determine where your team is currently at, and how we can help you elevate your team to the next level

About Perch

The People Experience and Impact Company.

A strong Operating System is critical to the success of all organizations.

We started Perch to help startups and small enterprises scale and run their organizations more efficiently, as we have seen many of them struggle to bring their own teams and resources onto the same business cadence.

Our experience working at startups and large corporations have given us key insights into how various companies designed their Operating Systems, and why some are successful while some are not.

Our Core Belief: People and Impact are key to successful organizations, and we want to be there to help you build an enduring one.
